About Razma

Razma is a picturesque mountain village nestled in the Albanian Alps, known for its pristine nature, traditional hospitality, and excellent hiking opportunities. Located at an altitude of approximately 900 meters, it offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.

The area is characterized by its rich biodiversity, pine forests, and traditional alpine architecture, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful mountain retreat.
