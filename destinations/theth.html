About Theth

Theth is a remote mountain village nestled in the heart of the Albanian Alps, offering visitors an authentic glimpse into traditional Albanian mountain life. This pristine valley, surrounded by dramatic peaks and waterfalls, is part of the larger Theth National Park and represents one of Albania's most spectacular natural landscapes.

The village has preserved its traditional architecture, with characteristic stone houses and the iconic Lock-in Tower, while providing a perfect base for hiking adventures and cultural experiences in the Albanian Alps.
