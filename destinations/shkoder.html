About Shkodër

Shkodër, one of Albania's oldest and most historic cities, stands as a cultural bridge between the coastal plains and the Albanian Alps. With over 2,500 years of history, it represents a fascinating blend of Ottoman, Venetian, and Albanian heritage.

The city is known for its rich cultural life, traditional crafts, and as the gateway to the spectacular northern mountains. Its position near Lake Shkodër, the largest lake in Southern Europe, adds to its natural beauty.
