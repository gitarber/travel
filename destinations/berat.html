About Berat

Berat, known as the "City of a Thousand Windows," is one of Albania's most beautiful and historic cities. This UNESCO World Heritage site showcases a unique blend of Ottoman and Albanian architecture, with white houses climbing up the hill towards Berat Castle. The city's history spans over 2,400 years, making it one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in Albania.

The city is divided into three main historic districts: Mangalem, Gorica, and Kalaja (the Castle), each offering its own unique charm and architectural significance.
