About Valbona

Valbona Valley National Park, often called the "Miracle of the Alps," is one of Albania's most spectacular natural areas. The valley, carved by the crystal-clear Valbona River, is surrounded by jagged limestone peaks reaching heights over 2,500 meters.

This pristine wilderness offers visitors a perfect blend of dramatic mountain scenery, traditional village life, and world-class hiking opportunities. The park is part of the larger Prokletije mountain range, also known as the Albanian Alps.
