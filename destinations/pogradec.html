About Pogradec

Pogradec is a picturesque lakeside city nestled on the shores of Lake Ohrid, one of Europe's oldest and deepest lakes. Known for its stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and unique ecosystem, Pogradec offers visitors a perfect blend of relaxation and exploration.

The city is famous for its pristine beaches, the enchanting Drilon Springs, and its delicious local cuisine, particularly the Koran fish found only in Lake Ohrid.
