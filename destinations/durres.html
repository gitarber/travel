About Durrës

Durrës is one of Albania's oldest cities, known for its rich history and beautiful beaches. It features a blend of ancient Roman ruins and modern attractions, making it a popular destination for tourists.

The city is famous for its Roman Amphitheatre, which is one of the largest in the Balkans, and offers a glimpse into the region's historical significance.
