About Koman Lake

Koman Lake is a man-made reservoir that has become one of Albania's most spectacular natural attractions. The ferry journey through its dramatic fjord-like landscape is often compared to the Norwegian fjords, offering breathtaking views of steep cliffs, hidden waterfalls, and pristine nature.

Created by the Koman Hydroelectric Dam in 1978, the lake winds through deep gorges and mountains, providing a vital transport link between Koman and Fierza while offering visitors an unforgettable journey through some of Albania's most dramatic scenery.

Important Notes

  • Advance booking recommended
  • Arrive 30 mins before departure
  • Best as a day trip

Accommodation Note

Koman is best experienced as a day trip. We recommend staying in nearby Shkodër or Valbona, which offer a wide range of accommodation options. The ferry journey is timed perfectly for day visitors, allowing you to experience the spectacular journey and return the same day.
