Adventure & Outdoor

Experience Albania's breathtaking landscapes through thrilling activities

Hiking & Trekking

Explore pristine mountain trails in the Albanian Alps, including the famous Theth to Valbona hike and the Llogara Pass.

  • Difficulty: Moderate to Challenging
  • Best Season: May to October
  • Duration: 1-7 days

White Water Rafting

Challenge yourself on the wild waters of the Osumi Canyon, one of Europe's most spectacular rafting destinations.

  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Best Season: March to June
  • Duration: 1 day

Cycling Tours

Discover Albania's diverse landscapes on two wheels, from coastal routes to mountain trails.

  • Difficulty: Easy to Moderate
  • Best Season: April to October
  • Duration: 1-5 days

Cultural & Heritage

Immerse yourself in Albania's rich history and traditions

Cooking Classes

Learn to prepare traditional Albanian dishes with local chefs in authentic settings.

  • Type: Hands-on Workshop
  • Duration: 3-4 hours
  • Group Size: 2-8 people

Wine Tasting Tours

Discover Albania's ancient wine-making traditions and sample local varieties.

  • Type: Guided Tour
  • Duration: 2-3 hours
  • Location: Berat, Tirana

Traditional Festivals

Experience vibrant local festivals celebrating music, dance, and cultural heritage.

  • Season: Year-round
  • Duration: 1-3 days
  • Various Locations

Coastal & Marine

Explore Albania's stunning coastline and marine life

Scuba Diving

Discover underwater treasures in the crystal-clear waters of the Ionian Sea.

  • Type: Guided Dives
  • Best Season: May to October
  • Duration: 2-4 hours

Sea Kayaking

Paddle through hidden coves and explore secluded beaches along the coast.

  • Type: Guided Tour
  • Best Season: June to September
  • Duration: 2-6 hours

Traditional Fishing

Join local fishermen and learn traditional fishing techniques.

  • Type: Hands-on Experience
  • Best Season: April to October
  • Duration: 4-6 hours
